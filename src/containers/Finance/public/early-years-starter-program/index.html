Everything you’d want to know before calling us

  • - Who is ESP for?

    ESP is for pre-schools, private schools, parent groups, and individual parents who want to give their Nur - Gr 2 children a great learning opportunity through this lockdown.

  • - When/Where is this happening?

    First batch of ESP is well underway across 35 schools. Your batch can begin today from the comfort of your home.

  • - What is the duration of ESP?

    The bridge course will run for 6-8 weeks in April and May.

  • - How much is ESP?

    ESP is free. Yes, FREE. In these difficult times, we want you to focus on the learning. Not on the money.

Answer to some more questions you might have

  • - Who runs ESP ?

    ESP is being offered by K12 Techno Services, a school management company that runs 35 schools with 25K students across Bangalore, Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Kolkata, and Chennai

  • -Who are the teachers?

    Our teachers are very friendly, tech savvy, and well trained in handling young kids. They have years of classroom experience in Pre-primary.

  • - What happens if I miss a session?

    Your session material will land in your inbox for your child to try it out at home.

  • What do I need to take up ESP?

    A steady internet connection, a spare device (preferably a laptop) and a keen interest in working with your child through this lockdown
